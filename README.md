# EDGE Profile Builder

AI gives generic answers when it does not understand you.

EDGE Profile Builder helps you create a reusable `learner-profile.md` file that you can paste into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, or any other AI tool as personalised context.

It helps AI understand your:

- Environment — where you are right now
- Direction — where you are trying to go
- Gaps — what is blocking you
- Engagement — how you want AI to support you

---

## Why This Exists

Most people ask AI for help without giving it enough context.

They ask:

> Help me with my career.  
> Write this better.  
> Give me advice.  
> Help me decide.

But AI does not know your current situation, pressure, goals, blockers, working style, or preferred support style.

So the response becomes generic.

EDGE Profile Builder fixes that by helping you create a personal AI context file.

---

## What EDGE Means

### E — Environment

Where are you right now?

### D — Direction

Where are you trying to go?

### G — Gaps

What is blocking or slowing you down?

### E — Engagement

How should AI work with you?

---

## Output

The final output is:

```md
learner-profile.md
```

This is not a resume.

This is not a bio.

This is not a motivational identity statement.

It is a practical personalisation file for AI tools.

---

## How To Use Your learner-profile.md

Before asking AI for serious help, paste this:

```text
I am sharing my EDGE Profile below. Use this to understand my current environment, direction, gaps, and preferred engagement style. Do not give generic advice. Ask for missing context when needed. Push back on vague answers. Help me think clearly and take practical action.

[Paste learner-profile.md here]
```

Then ask your question.
