# FAQ

## Is this a resume?

No. This is not a resume. It is a personal AI context file.

## Can I use it with Claude or Gemini?

Yes. You can paste `learner-profile.md` into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, or other LLMs.

## Should I include private information?

Only include what you are comfortable sharing with AI tools. Avoid sensitive personal information unless truly necessary.

## How often should I update it?

Update it when your situation, goals, gaps, or working style changes.

## What if my answers are generic?

That is normal at first. The builder should push you to make them more specific.

## Can this be used by students, founders, freelancers, or professionals?

Yes. EDGE Profile Builder is generic and can be used by anyone who wants AI to understand them better.
